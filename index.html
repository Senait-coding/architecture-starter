What do you know about Belgium?

Here you will find 20 questions about this beautiful country. Each question has 4 possible answers. When you click on the answer that you consider correct, the color of the button containing your selection will turn green if the answer is correct, or red if the answer is incorrect.

It is not possible to return to the previous question. You must finish the questionnaire to know the results. After answering the last question, you will be able to know the number of correct and incorrect answers.
